Pasta béchamel

with modzarela , pickle and French fries

50$

A medium sized piece contains 400 calories

It contains a high percentage of fats, proteins, saturated fats, minerals and starches. It is rich in protein, which is 50 grams, dietary fiber is about 4 grams, and it has 240 mg sodium.

If you are on a diet, we replace the white pasta with brown pasta of 300 grams, then add a filling of minced meat or minced chicken and put it with onions, tomatoes, black pepper and turkey and paste them with only one drop of oil. To make the bechamel, starch or dark brown flour is also used and skimmed milk is used, so we get pasta with bechamel, its calories are much lower and suitable for dieting.

Goat meat

with BBQ sauce, pickle and French fries

100$

Goat meat contains 500 calories

Goat meat is a rich source of proteins that help maintain healthy muscles. The basic amino acid profile of goat meat is similar to that of chicken and beef. Since goat meat is a lean meat, it provides all the essential amino acids without adding unnecessary calories, unlike other meat sources.

Goat meat is red meat, which means that it is high in myoglobin. Meats rich in myoglobin provide a higher level of dietary iron and improve blood health. There is about 3 mg of dietary iron in 100 g of goat meat. Goat meat can reduce the risk of iron deficiency anemia
